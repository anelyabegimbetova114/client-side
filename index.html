Introduction to Apple

Founded in 1976, Apple Inc. is a global leader in consumer electronics, software, and digital services.

Products

Apple's product line includes the iPhone, iPad, Mac computers, Apple Watch, and Apple TV, known for their innovation and design.

Software and Services

Apple offers software solutions like iOS, macOS, and services like iCloud, Apple Music, and the App Store.

Future Vision

Apple continues to invest in AI, VR, and health tech, paving the way for new innovations in technology and user experience.

Apple's Environmental Initiatives

Apple is committed to reducing its environmental impact, with initiatives focused on renewable energy, recycling, and sustainability in production.
